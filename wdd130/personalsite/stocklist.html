VOO

VOO is a world-renowned ETF, issued by Vanguard (American Pioneer Group), which was established in 2010. Track the S&P500 index, which are the most famous and profitable large companies (large blue-chip stocks) in the U.S. market. The total number of constituent stocks is about 500, including well-known companies such as GOOGLE, Apple, Amazon, FB, Microsoft, Poksha and Jiaosheng. These stocks are considered to be quite safe, because these large companies are unlikely to go bankrupt. However, because the scale of these stocks is quite large, profits are unlikely to increase significantly, which is more suitable for investors who want stability to choose.

TESLA

Tesla, formerly known as Tesla Automobile, is the largest electric vehicle and solar panel company in the United States. It cooperates with Panasonic to produce and sell electric vehicles, on-board computers, solar panels and storage equipment and system solutions. Tesla is the earliest self-driving car manufacturer in the world. By 2018, Tesla has become the world's best-selling rechargeable car company. In October 2021, it became the sixth giant enterprise with a market value of more than $1 trillion.
